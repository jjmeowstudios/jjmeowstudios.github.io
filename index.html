Brain Puzzle: Block Game

Brain Puzzle: Block Game is a free block puzzle game that’s both fun and challenging. It’s not just about playing—it’s about testing your mind and skills. The goal is simple yet addictive: score as many points as possible by clearing blocks from the board.

Classic

Drag and drop blocks onto the board and match as many as possible in this challenging yet entertaining logic game. Be strategic—don’t just place pieces randomly. Analyze the available blocks, plan your moves, and maximize your points. Clear full rows and columns, chain combos, and boost your score. Compete with friends and climb the global leaderboard. This fast-paced mode is perfect for quick matches—whether you're on public transport, waiting for a friend, or just relaxing on the couch.

Puzzle

Step into a world of mind-bending puzzles and tricky challenges. Train your brain with logical block puzzles, sharpen your problem-solving skills, and push your mental limits. Complete as many puzzles as possible and rack up points on the global leaderboard. With five difficulty levels, there's a challenge for everyone—play at your own pace.
